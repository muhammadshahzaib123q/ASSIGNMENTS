Paris

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. Known for its romantic ambiance and beautiful architecture, Paris is one of the most visited cities in the world. Some of its most popular attractions include the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.
